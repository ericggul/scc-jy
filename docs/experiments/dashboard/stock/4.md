# Stock 4 — cat-panel variant

`/stock/4` is a hover-decomposition clone of `/stock/3`. At rest, the complete
workstation preserves the accepted `/stock/3` interface.

- Entering a decomposed parent replaces every child with a separate cat image.
- Only the exact child beneath the pointer runs the rapid sequence; its sibling
  cat images remain still. Moving away restores the original interface.
- Each photograph fills the active component geometry with a cover crop.
- No caption, panel header, or explanatory chrome is added.
- Every other workstation panel, interaction, data record, layout rule, and
  responsive behavior remains inherited from the accepted `/stock/3` baseline.
- The world-market-intensity implementation and `breadthGroups` model remain
  unchanged under `components/dashboard/stock/3`.

## Image sources and playback

The source ledger lives at
`components/dashboard/stock/4/model/cat-sources.json`. It records each Wikimedia Commons
description page and the search term used to find it. The 320-pixel derivatives
are stored under `public/images/stock-4/cats/` to keep the sequence intentionally
low-resolution and locally stable.

Playback follows the established Bastille Day mechanism: images decode
sequentially, only successful decodes enter the pool, the previous frame
remains visible while loading continues, a canvas applies a full cover crop,
and a resize observer redraws the current frame. The source description pages
are the authority for each image's author and license.

The upper-right terminal status area contains an embedded `CAT / KISS` switch.
`CAT` retains the original 20-image cat pool; `KISS` uses 62 locally stored,
low-resolution adult kissing, fetish, boudoir, and nude photographs from
`components/dashboard/stock/4/model/kiss-sources.json`. `KISS` is the initial
selection.

  only the directly hovered clock animates

  hovered block rotates every 20 ms while the other nineteen rotate every
  200 ms
- First Word: whole panel
- Economic Releases: whole panel
- Sovereign Markets: whole panel
- World Market Intensity: whole panel
- S&P Performance: whole panel
- My Security Selection: whole panel and each of its three sector groups
- Major Market Dashboard: whole panel and each of its eight market cards
- On Screen: whole panel
